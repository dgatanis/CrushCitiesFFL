Crush Cities FF

Welcome to the Crush Cities dynasty fantasy football league, founded in the summer of 2020. This league brings together 10 members dedicated to bringing home a jersey of their choice, each and every year (even with questionable roster management). At the conclusion of the season, the champion is given the opportunity to select our new destination to draft rookies that will almost certainly become future waiver additions for someone else. Luck? Skill? Who cares. The only thing that matters is: Don't. Finish. Last.
